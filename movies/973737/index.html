Bungo Stray Dogs the Movie: BEAST

Set in an alternative world of Bungo Stray Dogs, this "what-if" story imagines if Atsushi Nakajima was in the Port Mafia and Rynosuke Akutagawa was in the Armed Detective Agency, instead of vice-versa. The story follows Rynosuke Akutagawa, who vows revenge on a man dressed in black in order to rescue his sister. However, as he is about to starve to death, a man from the Armed Detective Agency appears.
